CloudySky does not collect any personal information when you use the App. The App functions solely based on the city name you enter to provide weather information.

CloudySky does not access or collect your device's location information. The App relies on the city name you input to fetch the relevant weather data.

To provide accurate weather information, CloudySky may use third-party weather data providers. CloudySky may utilize third-party analytics services to gather non-personal information, such as App usage statistics, error reports, and general demographics. These services help us understand how users interact with the App, enabling us to improve its functionality and user experience. The information collected by these services is aggregated, anonymized, and does not identify individual users.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently gathered personal information from a child under 13, we will take prompt steps to delete such information from our records.
